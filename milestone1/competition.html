The Competitors

Intermountain Windows and Doors LLC

Content - Has a bar at the top that immediately provides contact information. I don’t love where it’s placed but I do like that it is accessible easily. Has the normal navigation bar. I do enjoy that, it makes it easy to find.

Images - Photos are emotionally provoking. That’s a good feature. Also has a bar showing what kind of work that they do. Nice.

Color Scheme - Red, white, and grey. Felt pretty neutral with a pop of color.

Layout - Very blocky. Like one block per screen.

All Star Pros

Content - Has a nav bar that disappears as you move the screen. Don’t love. All of the contact information first in a way that reminds me of a document. Not a fan. Shows some of the customer reviews, I do really like that. Shows you what kind of windows they support. Love that!

Images - Showing what they provide. Has pictures with smiling previous customers, pulls the emotional card.

Color Scheme - Blue, black, and white. A bit of a darker neutral tone. Was appealing to the eye.

Layout - Felt like I was looking at a document for the top half of the screen. Was extremely confused to find that the rest of the page looked quite great.
